Instagram Contest Rules

To win a contest offered by Instagram user @dailyiqchallenge, a participant must post his or her answer to the contest post comments. For a participant to be declared the winner, the participant's answer must:
1. Be correct as determined by @dailyiqchallenge

2. Contain an adequate explation of the rationale behind the answer. Any rationale that is of equal or lesser complexity to the rationale used to formulate the question may be considered correct, as determined by @dailyiqchallenge. In other words, any rationale that is correct and reasonably simple will likely be considered correct.

3. Be posted prior to any other correct answer. There will never be more than one winner per contest.

4. Be posted within 24 hours of the contest post. The contest expires after 24 hours.
After 24 hours, @dailyiqchallenge will announce the winner, if any, and contact the winning participant via DM. Contest winnings can be paid through common payment providers such as PayPal, Venmo, etc.

Instagram user @dailyiqchallenge holds the ultimately authority in determining winners and payment eligibility. These rules are subject to change. Most importantly:
HAVE FUN!
