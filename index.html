Instagram Contest Rules

To win a contest offered by @dailyiqchallenge, a participant must post his or her answer to the comment section of the contest post. For a participant to be declared the winner, the participant's comment answer must:
1. Be correct as determined by @dailyiqchallenge.

2. If specified by @dailyiqchallenge in the contest post, contain a correct and adequate explanation of the rationale behind the answer. Correctness and adequacy will be determined solely by @dailyiqchallenge.

3. Be posted prior to any other correct answer. Unless otherwise stated by @dailyiqchallenge in the contest post, there will never be more than one winner per contest.

4. Be posted within 24 hours of the contest post. The contest expires after 24 hours.

5. Contain tags of five (5) unique Instagram users, not including the participant user and @dailyiqchallenge.

6. Meet any other requirements specified by @dailyiqchallenge in the contest post, including any requirements that may differ from those above.
After 24 hours, @dailyiqchallenge will announce the winner, if any, and contact the winning participant via DM. Contest winnings can be paid through common payment providers such as PayPal, Venmo, etc. for the amount announced by @dailyiqchallenge in the contest post.

Notwithstanding the above rules, Instagram user @dailyiqchallenge has ultimate authority in determining winners and is not liable to contest participants under any circumstances. These rules are subject to change. Most importantly:
HAVE FUN!
